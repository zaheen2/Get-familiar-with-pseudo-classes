YouTube is the world's largest video-sharing platform, allowing users to upload, watch, and engage with content across various genres. Founded in 2005, it has become a hub for entertainment, education, and creativity, where anyone can find or share videos on nearly any topic. From music and vlogs to tutorials and documentaries, YouTube connects billions of people globally, enabling creators to showcase their work and audiences to discover new content. Its vast library and easy accessibility make it a go-to source for both casual viewers and serious learners.
